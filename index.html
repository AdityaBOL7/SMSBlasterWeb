About

Built for businesses and marketers who send at scale.

SMS Blaster is a powerful Android application designed for businesses and marketers who need to send bulk messages at scale. With support for both traditional SMS and modern RCS messaging, SMS Blaster automates your entire campaign workflow, from fetching contacts to sending messages to tracking delivery, all from your Android phone.

Features

SMS and RCS in one workflow, synced with your server.

Dual-Channel Messaging

Send via SMS or RCS from a single app. The app automatically detects RCS availability for each contact and picks the best channel.

Bulk Campaigns

Send to thousands of numbers in one go. Fetch campaigns from your server, and the app handles queuing, sending, and tracking automatically.

Smart Throttling

Built-in intelligent rate limiting prevents your messages from getting blocked. Three modes:

  • Conservative: safe and steady
  • Balanced: optimal speed and safety
  • Aggressive: maximum throughput

Media Attachments

Send images and media alongside your messages through the RCS channel. Not just plain text.

Real-Time Delivery Tracking

Know exactly which messages were sent and delivered. Per-number status tracking with full reporting synced back to your server.

Background Operation

Runs as a foreground service. It keeps sending even when your screen is off or you switch to another app. Set it and forget it.

Multi-SIM Support

Running multiple SIMs? The app tracks each SIM separately with independent quotas and queues.

Server-Managed Campaigns

Connect to your campaign server. The app fetches phone numbers, sends messages, and reports delivery status, all automatically. No manual work.

How It Works

Configure, fetch, send, and track, tied to your server.

  1. 1

    Configure

    Enter your server details, credentials, and choose SMS or RCS.

  2. 2

  3. 3

    Send

    Hit start. Messages go out automatically in smart batches.

  4. 4

    Track

    Monitor delivery in real-time. Reports sync back to your server.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

SMS Blaster is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Information We Collect

Device Information

  • Device IMEI and SIM serial number (used for device identification and campaign management)
  • Battery status (used to monitor device health during operation)
  • Device model and Android version

Account Information

  • Username and password (used for server authentication)
  • Device number identifier

Messaging Data

  • Phone numbers received from your campaign server (used solely for message delivery)
  • Message content received from your campaign server
  • Delivery status reports (sent, delivered, failed)

Permissions Used

  • SMS permissions: to send and track SMS messages
  • Phone state: to read SIM and device identifiers
  • Storage: to handle media attachments for RCS messages
  • Internet: to communicate with your campaign server
  • Accessibility Service: to automate RCS message sending through Google Messages

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your device with the campaign server
  • To fetch and deliver messaging campaigns
  • To track and report delivery status
  • To manage per-device and per-SIM quotas

Data Storage

  • Account credentials are stored locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences
  • Message queues and delivery reports are stored in a local Room database on your device
  • Campaign data is synced between your device and your campaign server

Data Sharing

  • We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties
  • Message delivery data (delivery reports, SIM info) is sent only to your own configured campaign server
  • No data is sent to any analytics or advertising platforms

Data Retention

  • Local message data and delivery reports are stored on your device until cleared by the app or user
  • Server-side data retention is governed by your own campaign server's policies

Security

  • All server communication uses HTTP/HTTPS protocols
  • Credentials are stored locally on-device only
  • No cloud backup of sensitive data

Children's Privacy

SMS Blaster is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

🧾 Account & Data Deletion

Overview

We respect your privacy and give you full control over your data. You can request deletion of your account and all associated information at any time.

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your account and data:

  • Email us at SHASHANK@BOL7.COM
  • Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Include your registered username and device number for verification

Your request will be processed within 7 business days.

What Data We Delete

Once your request is verified and processed, we permanently delete:

  • Your account credentials (username and password)
  • Campaign data and delivery reports
  • Device-related information (Device ID, SIM details)
  • Any personally identifiable information stored on our servers

Data Stored on Your Device

The app may store the following data locally on your device:

  • Login credentials (stored in SharedPreferences)
  • Device ID, IMEI, SIM number
  • Campaign data and delivery reports (Room database)
  • Call forwarding settings

How to Delete Data from Your Device

You can remove all locally stored data instantly by:

  1. Going to Settings
  2. Opening Apps → SMS Blaster
  3. Selecting Storage → Clear Data
  4. Uninstalling the app

Important Notes

  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • Some minimal logs may be retained temporarily for security or legal purposes
  • If you face any issues, contact support
